Research Interests

My primary research interests lie in the areas of integrable systems, supersymmetry, and string theory. I am particularly focused on exploring integrable sigma models, their deformations, and their connections to higher-dimensional gauge theories, such as 4d Chern-Simons theory. My work involves studying the mathematical structures underlying these models, including quantum groups and Yang-Baxter equations, as well as investigating their physical implications.
